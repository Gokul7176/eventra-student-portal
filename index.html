About Portal

Eventra Overview

Eventra is a unified Student Event Registration Portal built for the CIT campus. It simplifies event hosting and participation by providing a single, streamlined registration desk for fests, sports meets, cultural celebrations, hackathons, and academic workshops. With Eventra, students can easily manage their registrations in one place, and club organizers get real-time dashboards to manage RSVP queues, confirmations, and attendance tracking.

Help & Support

Frequently asked questions about the Eventra portal

How do I register for an event?

To register for an event, scroll to the Registration section, fill in your personal details (Name, Email, Phone, Age, DOB, and preferred time), select your skills/interests, and write comments. Click the Submit Registration button. Once validated, your profile will be created, and you can participate in campus events.

How does Drag and Drop work?

In the Drag & Drop Event Category section, you can click and hold any of the three event category cards (Technical, Sports, or Cultural) and drag them over the dashed drop zone. Releasing the mouse button will drop the card, which automatically selects that category and syncs it with the Preferred Event Category in the Web Storage section.

What is the difference between Local Storage and Session Storage?

Both are HTML5 Web Storage mechanisms, but they differ in data persistence:

  • Local Storage: Data remains stored in the user's browser indefinitely, even after the browser is closed or restarted.
  • Session Storage: Data is stored only for the duration of the current browser tab session. Once the tab or browser is closed, the data is completely cleared.
