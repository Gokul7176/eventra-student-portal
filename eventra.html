What you can do

  • One-tap RSVP with instant seat confirmation
  • Live seat-availability counter on every event page
  • QR-code entry pass generated automatically
  • Automated reminder email 24 hours before an event
  • Digital participation certificate after check-in
  • Organizer view for attendance and feedback tracking

How registration works

  1. Browse upcoming events by department or club
  2. Register with your student details
  3. Pay the event fee, if any, through a secure gateway
  4. Receive your QR ticket by email
  5. Check in by scanning the QR code at the venue
  6. Download your certificate after the event
